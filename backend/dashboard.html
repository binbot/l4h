Welcome

What a great day to look at applications! Right?

Be sure that you or a fellow team member checks the applications for your branch frequently. If an application meets your criteria, mark it as Accepted. An email will be sent to the applicant automatically. Marking applications as Denied works on the same principle. It is a good idea to call the applicant by phone if you do not receive further correspondence through email.

When you deliver a computer to an accepted applicant, please mark it as Delivered. Thanks!
